Questions?

Most frequently asked ones
What could be communication channels?
We communicate via email, Slack, WhatsApp, and scheduled video calls. We adapt to your preferred workflow and ensure consistent updates throughout the project.
How does your process work?
Pure, purposeful digital experiences. We provide thoughtful design and smart features that streamline your workflow and drive long-term results. Five clear stages: Discover → Analysis → Strategy → Execution → Optimization.
How involved do I need to be?
We can work with as little or as much involvement as you prefer. At minimum, we need your input at key milestones — kick-off, mid-review, and final delivery.
How flexible is your revision policy?
All projects include a structured revision cycle. We offer two rounds of revisions as standard, with additional rounds available depending on the project scope.
What happens after delivery?
We offer post-launch support packages to handle maintenance, updates, and ongoing optimization. You're never left on your own after delivery.
How secure is your data handling?
We follow industry best practices for data security, including NDAs, encrypted communications, and strict access controls throughout every project.
