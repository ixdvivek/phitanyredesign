Story

Founded in 2012, Phitany Business Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a technology and digital growth company built on strategic thinking and execution precision.

Over the years, we have partnered with businesses across industries to design scalable digital ecosystems — integrating technology, branding, marketing intelligence, and automation into unified growth frameworks.

Path To Innovation

Our journey of 14 years

2012

The Beginning

Founded with a vision to create intelligent, performance-driven digital solutions.

Laid foundations for our first digital product in the e-commerce sector.

Secured initial funds and built a strong and dedicated team.

2016

Scaling Up

Expanded into full-service digital engineering and brand strategy.

Opened our first dedicated studio in Cochin.

Crossed 100 completed client projects.

2019

Going Further

Built our performance-marketing and analytics practice.

Partnered with brands across India and the GCC.

Grew the team to 30+ specialists.

2020

Resilience

Shifted to a remote-first, distributed delivery model.

Launched automation and CRM solutions for clients.

Doubled our recurring retainer partnerships.

2023

New Horizons

Integrated AI into our engineering and growth workflows.

Surpassed 460 projects delivered worldwide.

Recognised among Kerala's leading digital studios.
